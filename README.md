# Cheese Out!

This game is still on it's early phase, please come check later! 

Protect your cheese from the eager mices! In the alternative of the popular game "Breakout" you play against your friend or the computer to eat their cheese and protect yours from them. 

## Deploy on Itch.io
Go try the game here! : [Itch.io](https://spykielucilius.itch.io/cheese-out)
## Features
* **Local Multiplayer:** Play against a friend on the same keyboard.
* **VS Computer:** Play against the computer.

## Controls
* **Player 1 (bottom):**  `A` (Left) / `D` (Right)
* **Player 2 (top):**     `Left Arrow` (Left) / `Right Arrow` (Right)

## How to Play
1. Each player controls a paddle on opposite sides of the arena.
2. Deflect the mices to smash your opponent's cheese tiles.
3. Block the mices to protect your own cheese.
4. The first player to destroy all of the opponent's cheese wins!
